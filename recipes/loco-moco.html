Loco Moco

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Ground Beef
  • Mushrooms
  • Beef Broth
  • Beef Broth
  • Shoyu
  • Worcestershire Sauce
  • Cornstarch

Steps

  1. Combine ground beef with chopped onion, garlic, Worcestershire sauce, salt, and pepper in a mixing bowl.
  2. Shape meat into 4 equal-sized patties and refrigerate until you're ready to cook them.
  3. Caramelized onion wedges in a little oil in your skillet. Set aside for gravy.
  4. Cook burgers in skillet.
  5. Saute sliced cremini mushrooms in skillet until tender. Add caramelized onion wedges, beef broth, shoyu, and Worcestershire sauce.
  6. Thicken gravy with a bit of cornstarch.
  7. Fry an egg sunny side up.
  8. Place rice on a plate followed by hamburger and egg. Pour gravy on top.
