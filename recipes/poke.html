Ahi Poke

Cubed Ahi with spicy mayo sauce, green onions, and sesame seeds

Ingredients

  • Sashimi grade Ahi
  • 1/4 cup Kewpie mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons Sriracha
  • 1 teaspoon Shoyu
  • 1/2 teaspoon Sesame Oil
  • 2 teaspoons thinly sliced Green onion

Steps

  1. Add mayonnaise, sriracha, shoyu, and sesame oil to boil. Mix well.
  2. Slice ahi into small cubes about 3/4 inch thick.
  3. Place ahi cubes into a bowl and add spicy mayo mix along with green onion. Gently toss to coat.
