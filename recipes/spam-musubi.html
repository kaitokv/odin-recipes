Spam Musubi

Glazed Spam with rice wrapped in nori

Ingredients

  • Spam
  • Shoyu
  • Mirin
  • Sugar
  • Rice
  • Nori
  • Furikake

Steps

  1. Cut spam into 6 even slices.
  2. Fry spam on medium heat until crispy.
  3. Mix together shoyu, mirin, Sugar.
  4. Turn heat to low and pour glaze onto spam.
  5. Use about 2/3 cups of cooked rice per spam musubi and mold rice into shape.
  6. Pour furikake onto top of molded rice.
  7. Place one slice of spam onto the molded rice.
  8. Wrap nori around spam and rice.
  9. Repeat steps 5 through 8 for each slice of spam.
